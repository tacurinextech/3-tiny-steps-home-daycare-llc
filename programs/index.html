Our Programs

We offer age-appropriate programs designed to nurture each child's development at their own pace.

Infant Care

6 weeks - 18 months

Nurturing care for our youngest children with personalized attention to support their developmental milestones.

  • Low child-to-caregiver ratio (3:1)
  • Daily reports on feeding, napping, and activities
  • Tummy time and sensory play
  • Soothing environment for optimal development

Toddler Program

18 months - 3 years

Active learning experiences that encourage exploration, language development, and social skills.

  • Potty training support
  • Language-rich environment
  • Fine and gross motor skill activities
  • Introduction to routines and group play

Preschool

3 - 5 years

Comprehensive curriculum preparing children for kindergarten through play-based learning.

  • Pre-reading and math foundations
  • Social-emotional development
  • Creative arts and science exploration
  • Kindergarten readiness activities

Daily Schedule

Our balanced daily routine provides structure while allowing flexibility for children's individual needs.

8:00 - 8:30

Arrival & Free Play

Children arrive and engage in quiet activities as they transition into the day.

8:30 - 9:00

Breakfast

Nutritious morning meal served family-style to promote social skills.

9:00 - 9:30

Morning Circle

Group time for songs, stories, and discussion of the day's activities.

9:30 - 10:30

Learning Activities

Age-appropriate educational activities and projects.

10:30 - 11:30

Outdoor Play

Fresh air and physical activity in our secure playground.

11:30 - 12:15

Lunch

Balanced, home-cooked meal served family-style.

12:15 - 2:30

Nap/Quiet Time

Rest period with soft music and individual cots.

2:30 - 3:00

Afternoon Snack

Healthy snack to refuel after nap time.

3:00 - 4:30

Afternoon Activities

Art, music, or science projects followed by free play.

4:30 - 5:00

Evening Wind-Down

Quiet activities and preparation for pickup.
