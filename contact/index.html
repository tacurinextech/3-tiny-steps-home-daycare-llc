Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our daycare services.

What are your hours of operation?

We are open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on major holidays and have limited hours during holiday weeks.

What is your staff-to-child ratio?

Our staff-to-child ratios exceed state requirements. For infants, we maintain a 1:3 ratio, toddlers 1:4, and preschoolers 1:6.

Do you provide meals and snacks?

Yes, we provide breakfast, lunch, and two snacks daily. All meals are prepared fresh and follow USDA nutritional guidelines.

What is your illness policy?

Children must be fever-free for 24 hours without medication before returning to daycare. We also have policies for other contagious conditions to ensure everyone's health and safety.

How do you handle discipline?

We use positive guidance techniques including redirection, natural consequences, and encouragement of appropriate behaviors. We never use physical punishment or harsh language.

What security measures do you have in place?

Our facility has secure entry with a keypad system, all staff are background checked, and we have comprehensive emergency procedures in place.
